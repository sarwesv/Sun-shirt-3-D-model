Core Technologies

Chest
Medical-Grade ECG
Textile silver yarns woven directly into the front panel make continuous skin contact. The on-board signal processor filters motion artifact in real time, delivering ECG quality that matches clinical Holter monitors.

Sleeve Seams
Serpentine Data Tracks
Conductive silver-ink traces printed in a serpentine (S-curve) topology accommodate up to 40% elongation before any resistance change. Data and power travel from chest to wrist without wires or connectors.

Wrist Cuff
Stealth Fiber Optic Display
A 16 × 8 micro-LED matrix hidden beneath a polymer diffuser is invisible until activated. A flick-of-the-wrist gesture wakes the display; a second flick dismisses it—average on-time is 2.4 seconds.
🧠
Waistband
Flexible Brain Pod
A 3 mm-thick silicone enclosure houses the ARM Cortex-M33 MCU, BLE 5.3 radio, and a 220 mAh solid-state battery. The pod snaps magnetically onto the shirt's contacts and separates cleanly before the wash cycle.

FAQ

Remove the waistband pod by pinching and pulling—it detaches magnetically in under a second. The rest of the shirt, including all electronics, is fully IP68-sealed and machine-washable up to 40 °C. Simply toss it in like any other athletic shirt.
The textile electrode array captures a Lead I-equivalent ECG at ±2 bpm accuracy. An on-chip motion-artifact rejection algorithm filters noise from movement. The system is not a medical device—it is a wellness monitor and should not replace clinical assessment.
No. The wrist cuff display is entirely invisible at rest. The micro-LEDs are shielded behind a polymer diffuser that only becomes transparent when the LEDs are powered. In off state the wrist cuff looks and feels identical to any other performance sleeve.
Stealth Mode is a low-power state where all visible indicators turn off. The pod continues sampling biometrics and logging data locally, but the wrist display and BLE broadcast go dark. Battery life extends to 36 hours in this state.
Phase Change Material microcapsules are embedded in the fiber matrix. At your skin's heat threshold they absorb energy by melting (solid → liquid), buffering your microclimate by up to ±4 °C. The effect lasts roughly 45–90 minutes of intense activity before the PCMs are fully charged and need to re-solidify (typically during rest).
